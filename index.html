About Marathon

RENEWEL HELPS Win the race against time for those who need a kidney. Join us in the race to support this life saving project.

Come be a part of TEAM RENEWAL in the 2019 FULL MARATHON AND HALF MARATHON. The Miami Marathon will take place on SUNDAY JANUARY 27, 2019.

If you will be joining us from out of town, we will be glad to help you find accommodations for Shabbos. On Motzei Shabbos we will enjoy a pre-marathon Melave Malka.

At that time, you will receive your Official 2019 Team Renewal Marathon T-Shirt and Bib Number along with all of the relevant

Race instructions and information

In order to register as an official runner,
go to the web site www.themiamimarathon.com

Fundraising

2019 Team Renewal Miami Marathon: Spreading awareness for becoming a Living Kidney Donor and supporting this incredible cause.

Every Team Member will aspire to raise 1,800* up to the date of the Marathon.

100% of your fundraising efforts will go directly to Renewal to assure that another Live Donor will have the ability to succeed on their personal, unique journey towards saving a life.

2019 Team Renewal Miami Marathon: *You will not be charged if you are not successful in attaining your goal.
